Game 5: Knicks vs Celtics – Can New York Take Over?

Knicks fans are fired up for tonight’s crucial Game 5 matchup against the Celtics. But they might catch a break — Jayson Tatum is officially out with an Achilles injury.

Oddsmakers still like Boston (-5.5), but don’t count out New York. The Garden will be electric.

Bet of the Night: Knicks Moneyline.

Player to Watch: Payton Pritchard. With Tatum out, Payton could step up like he did in Game 3. Also, look for Finals MVP Jaylen Brown to take on a massive load.

Looking Ahead

The winner of this series will face Tyrese Haliburton and the red-hot Pacers, who have quietly turned into a playoff buzzsaw. Can either Boston or New York keep up with their pace?
