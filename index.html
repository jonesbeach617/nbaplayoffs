Game 5: Knicks vs Celtics – A Showdown in the Garden

All eyes are on Madison Square Garden tonight as the Knicks face off against the Celtics in a crucial Game 5. With the series tied and tensions high, this one has all the makings of a classic.

Breaking News: Jayson Tatum is OUT with an Achilles injury.

With Boston’s superstar sidelined, the Knicks have a real shot to take control. Oddsmakers still have the Celtics as -5.5 favorites, but momentum could shift quickly in New York’s favor.

Bet of the Night: Knicks Moneyline — with home crowd energy and Tatum out, this could be the value play.

Player Spotlight: Payton Pritchard — Expect an expanded role and minutes with Tatum unavailable. He was instrumental in Game 3 and could be Boston’s X-factor tonight.

Don't count out Jaylen Brown either — the reigning Finals MVP will need to shoulder the scoring load.
