Thunder vs Nuggets: Western War

Game Breakdown by Yoni

Jokic’s Brilliance Overshadowed

Once again, Nikola Jokic put on a clinic — his passing, footwork, and touch were all elite. But it was all spoiled by Michael Porter Jr.’s disappearing act. MPJ couldn’t find a rhythm, and his cold shooting cost the Nuggets momentum.

Where’s the Swagger, Shai?

Shai Gilgeous-Alexander has been solid this postseason, but fans are starting to wonder: where’s that MVP-caliber swagger from the regular season? He’s scoring — but he’s not commanding games like he used to. That must change if OKC wants to pull off the upset.

Jamal Murray’s Vintage Third Quarter

Murray erupted in the third — a reminder of his 2020 Bubble brilliance. But he cooled off down the stretch, and Denver couldn’t close. The Nuggets need full-game consistency from their backcourt to win the series.

Unsung Hero Watch: Isiah Hartenstein

Quietly, Isiah Hartenstein has been one of the best glue guys of the playoffs. His rebounding, interior presence, and hustle are key for the Knicks — and go beyond the box score.
